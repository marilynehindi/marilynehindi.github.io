Dolce & Gabbana

Novembre 2017

University project SPD

Dolce & Gabbana is a luxury Italian fashion house founded in 1985 in Milano by Italian designers Domenico Dolce and Stefano Gabbana.

The target of this project was to create a service and design an in-store mobile app.
