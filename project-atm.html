ATM Milano

February 2017

University group project SPD

ATM Milano is the public company owned by the City of Milan which administers and manages public transport in the Lombardy capital. ATM provides surface transport service by bus, metro and tram.

The goal of this project was to Review and re-design the ATM Kiosk and mobile App user interface and experience in order to make it more usable. we created an app and did the prototyping on in-vision.
