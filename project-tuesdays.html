Tuesdays Magazine

March 2017

University Project SPD

Tuesdays is a fashion digital magazine that talks to a young audience who loves fashion and consumes it through smartphones and the web.

The target of this project was to design an identity for “TUESDAYS” starting from typography only. I had to create a typographic logo, a table of content, an editor's letter, a fashion story, an editorial interview, and the layout of a quote.
