La Pedagogie De La Memoire Au Liban

August 2016

Freelance Project

La pedagogie de la memoire au Liban, is a book written in French language which aims to overflow the general and descriptive analyzes of the wars in Lebanon and collective memory and shares, undertaking a pedagogical experimentation of field with young Lebanese students. This book was written by Lamia Maurice Hitti.

I made the design of the cover by responding to the client's request without forgetting the awareness of the subject.
